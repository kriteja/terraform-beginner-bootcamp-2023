How to Make Chocolate Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chocolate ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Place the chocolate ice cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the milkshake into a glass and serve immediately.
